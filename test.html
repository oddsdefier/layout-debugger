How to Use the Debugger

  • Click the extension icon to open the sidebar panel
  • Click "Show Borders" to activate the debugger
  • All elements will show colored borders based on their classes
  • An inspection sidebar will appear on the page
  • Click any element to see its detailed properties
  • Click "Hide Borders" to turn off the debugger
