# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in Layout Debugger respectful, inclusive, and free from harassment for everyone, regardless of experience level, identity, background, or ability.

## Expected behavior

Examples of behavior that helps the project:

- Be respectful and specific when giving feedback.
- Assume good intent while discussing code or design decisions.
- Welcome questions from new contributors.
- Focus criticism on the work, not the person.
- Accept maintainer decisions when a discussion has reached its conclusion.

## Unacceptable behavior

The following behavior is not accepted:

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Publishing another person’s private information without permission.
- Deliberate disruption, spam, or repeated bad-faith arguments.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional community.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that violate this policy. Temporary or permanent participation restrictions may be applied when necessary.

To report a conduct issue, contact the maintainer privately using a contact method listed on the maintainer’s GitHub profile. Do not open a public issue containing sensitive details.

Reports will be reviewed as privately and promptly as reasonably possible. Retaliation against someone who reports a concern is not permitted.

## Scope

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone is publicly representing the project.

## Attribution

This policy is informed by the Contributor Covenant and other established open-source community guidelines.
